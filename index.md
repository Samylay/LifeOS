# lifeOS

Your second brain for all aspects of life.

## Structure

- **01-Inbox** - Quick captures, things to process
- **02-Knowledge** - Notes, learnings, references
- **03-Projects** - Active projects with defined outcomes
- **04-Goals** - Short-term and long-term goals
- **05-Areas** - Ongoing life areas (health, finance, career, etc.)
- **06-Resources** - People, tools, references
- **07-Templates** - Reusable note templates
- **08-Archives** - Completed/archived material
